Located in the heart of Chunagad, Ottapalam, Sankar's Furniture has been synonymous with exceptional craftsmanship and trust. We understand that furniture is more than just wood—it is the soul of your home.

Our philosophy is built entirely around customer needs. We specialize in creating and curating premium wooden furniture, stairs, doors, and authentic heritage maintenance. By offering quality products tailored to your distinct taste, we help you build spaces you'll love for generations.
